Features

Built for privacy, designed for people

Everything you love about modern chat, without compromising your data.

🛡

Quantum-Proof E2EE

Hybrid X25519 + ML-KEM-768 key exchange with AES-256-GCM. Your DMs and voice calls are protected against both classical and future quantum attacks.

🏛

Servers & Channels

Create servers with text and voice channels, roles, permissions, threads, and polls. Everything you expect from a modern chat platform.

🔍

Zero Data Collection

No tracking, no ads, no data mining. Source-available for full transparency. You own your data, always.

✅

Key Verification

Verify contacts with safety numbers. Compare fingerprints to ensure nobody is intercepting your messages.

⚡

Blazing Fast

Built with Rust on the backend. Real-time messaging via WebSocket with NATS event bus for instant delivery.

📱

Multi-Platform

Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and web. Your encrypted conversations sync across all your devices.
